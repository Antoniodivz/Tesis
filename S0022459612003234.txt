Nicola A. Spaldin,
A beginner's guide to the modern theory of polarization,
Journal of Solid State Chemistry,
Volume 195,
2012,
Pages 2-10,
ISSN 0022-4596,
https://doi.org/10.1016/j.jssc.2012.05.010.
(https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0022459612003234)
Abstract: The so-called Modern Theory of Polarization, which rigorously defines the spontaneous polarization of a periodic solid and provides a route for its computation in electronic structure codes through the Berry phase, is introduced in a simple qualitative discussion.
Keywords: Polarization; Berry phase; Electronic structure calculation
